CONSTRUCTION SPECIFICATIONS - PROJECT ALPHA

1. CONCRETE SPECIFICATIONS
   - Grade: M25 for foundations, M30 for columns
   - Cement: OPC 53 grade conforming to IS 12269
   - Aggregate: 20mm and 10mm graded as per IS 383
   - Water cement ratio: 0.45 maximum
   - Minimum curing period: 28 days

2. STEEL REINFORCEMENT
   - Grade: Fe 500 TMT bars conforming to IS 1786
   - Minimum cover: 40mm for foundations, 25mm for beams
   - Lap length: 40 times bar diameter
   - All bars to be rust-free and straight

3. BRICK WORK
   - Bricks: First class burnt clay bricks
   - Mortar: Cement sand mortar 1:6 ratio
   - Joints: 10-12mm thick, properly filled
   - Curing: Minimum 7 days after completion

4. FINISHING WORKS
   - Plastering: 12mm thick cement sand plaster 1:4
   - Painting: Two coats of primer plus two coats of emulsion
   - Flooring: Vitrified tiles 600x600mm with 2mm joints

5. QUALITY CONTROL
   - All materials to be tested before use
   - Concrete cube testing every 50 cum
   - Steel testing for every 10 tons
   - Regular supervision by qualified engineer
