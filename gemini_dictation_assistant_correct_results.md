### **1.11 QUALITY ASSURANCE**

This subsection outlines the stringent quality control measures and qualifications required for the wood doors <sup class="citation-marker" data-citation-index="0">1</sup>.

*   **Manufacturer Qualifications:** The manufacturers of wood doors must be certified according to WDMA quality standards. Additionally, they must be a certified participant in the Architectural Woodwork Institute's (AWI) Quality Certification Program <sup class="citation-marker" data-citation-index="0">1</sup>.
*   **Fire-Rated Doors:** All fire-rated doors situated in escape corridors are mandated to possess an integrity and insulation (EI) rating that is equivalent to the specified fire rating <sup class="citation-marker" data-citation-index="0">1</sup>.
*   **Mockups:** Before the installation of flush wood doors and frames, mockups are a prerequisite. These mockups serve to verify selections and to demonstrate the aesthetic quality and the execution of the work. Approval from the Engineer is compulsory before any custom fabrication can proceed <sup class="citation-marker" data-citation-index="0">1</sup>.
